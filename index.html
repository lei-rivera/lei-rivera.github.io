Hi, my name is Lei Rivera. I'm an entry-level software engineer specializing in website development. As a freelance web developer, my focus is on helping you reach more customers with a reliable online presence. I build clean, easy-to-use websites that highlight your business and make a strong first impression. While I specialize in simple static sites for now, I also have the mindset and skills to take on larger, more complex projects as the needs of your business grow. Whether it's for a simple landing page or a full-on web application down the road, I create solutions that are simple, efficient, and tailored to your goals. I'm quick to adapt, great at problem-solving, and I communicate clearly — so you can get a professional, polished site without the stress or confusing tech talk. My goal is to make the process easy and deliver a website that works for you and your customers.

A Natural Pest Solution

This project helped me to strengthen my front-end skills, improve my layout and design techniques, and deliver a website that builds trust and confidence in potential customers.
